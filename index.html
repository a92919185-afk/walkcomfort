Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Akusoli technology and relief.

What are Akusoli magnetic insoles?
Akusoli are premium orthotic inserts that combine centuries-old Japanese principles with modern magnetic design. Unlike regular foam insoles, they use magnets to provide a unique underfoot experience for natural, daily comfort.
How does Akusoli help with foot discomfort?
Akusoli is highly effective for everyday foot fatigue because it provides targeted arch support that reduces the strain on your feet. The magnetic points specifically aim to offer a comforting massage-like effect to keep you walking with ease.
Can Akusoli insoles improve posture?
Yes. Much of our daily bodily fatigue starts with poor foot alignment. Akusoli stabilizes your gait and aligns your ankles, which translates up your kinetic chain. This improved alignment can help support a more comfortable daily posture.
Will Akusoli fit my specific shoe size and type?
Absolutely. Akusoli insoles are designed with a universal trimmable template. We provide clearly marked cut-lines so you can use standard scissors to adjust the length for a perfect fit in sneakers, heavy work boots, or even formal shoes.
How long should I wear them to feel results?
While many feel immediate cushioning and support, your feet may need an adjustment period. We recommend wearing them regularly. Within 7 to 14 days, most users experience the full comfort benefits of the insoles.
Are Akusoli insoles breathable and antimicrobial?
Yes. Integrity of foot comfort is our priority. Akusoli uses moisture-wicking, breathable materials designed to keep your feet feeling fresh even after long, active days.
